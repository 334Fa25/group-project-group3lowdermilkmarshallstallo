Design & Planning Tips

  • Start with a plan: Sketch your yard layout first (sun exposure, shade, slopes, and wind patterns). It’ll help you choose the right plants for the right spots.
  • Create focal points: Use a statement piece like a tree, sculpture, or flower bed to draw attention and make the design cohesive.
  • Use the rule of three: Group plants in threes or odd numbers for a more natural, balanced look.
  • Think year-round: Combine plants that bloom in different seasons so something is always thriving.
  • Match your home’s style: Rustic home? Use natural stones and native plants. Modern home? Sleek edges and clean lines fit best.
